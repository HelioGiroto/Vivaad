Outline of spirituality Spirituality – can refer to an ultimate or an alleged
immaterial reality; an inner path enabling a person to discover the essence of
his/her being; or the “deepest values and meanings by which people live.”
Spiritual practices, including meditation, prayer and contemplation, are
intended to develop an individual's inner life; spiritual experience includes
that of connectedness with a larger reality, yielding a more comprehensive
self; with other individuals or the human community; with nature or the cosmos;
or with the divine realm.
