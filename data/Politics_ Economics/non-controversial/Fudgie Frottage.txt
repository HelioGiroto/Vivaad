Fudgie Frottage Fudgie Frottage () is the stage name of American drag
performer, producer and community activist Lu Read who is based in San
Francisco. Ms. Read, as Frottage, is well known in San Francisco's gay and
lesbian community as a club promoter and event producer. Read as Frottage also
emcees, produces and acts as a judge for local drag contests. Frottage is
