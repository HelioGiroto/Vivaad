Americentrism Americentrism is the idea or perceived bias to judge other
cultures and nations by US standards or to center around the US in news. It is
often confused with American exceptionalism, which is the idea that the US is
exceptional, better or worth more than other nations. The term refers to the
ethnocentric and xenophobic practice of viewing the world from an explicitly
American perspective, with an implied belief, either consciously or
subconsciously, in the preeminence of American culture. In the media. American
networks are said to have an americentric bias in the selection of their
material and U.S. celebrities have been accused of being "Americentric".
