2006 Franco–Italian–Spanish Middle East Peace Plan On 2006-11-16, France,
Italy and Spain announced a new Middle East peace plan proposed by Spanish
Premier José Luis Rodríguez Zapatero during talks with French president
Jacques Chirac. Later on, the plan was introduced to Romano Prodi, Italy's
prime minister who gave his full support to the plan. The peace move came after
Israel invaded the Gaza Strip in Operation Autumn Clouds.
