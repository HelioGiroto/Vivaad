The Family Foundation of Kentucky The Family Foundation of Kentucky is a non-
profit organization based in Lexington. Headed by Kent Ostrander, the
Foundation serves as a public policy on Kentucky issues affecting the family.
Though often sympathetic to causes important to religious conservatives, the
organization has no religious affiliation. Publications. The Foundation
publishes and distributes "The Kentucky Citizen", a bi-monthly newsletter.
Flood. On September 23, 2006, The Family Foundation's headquarters was
destroyed by a flood, costing them a number of irreplaceable documents and
data.
