Maternity Coalition The Maternity Coalition is an Australian advocacy group
which aims to improve the provision of maternity services to parents. It
consists of individuals as well as other non-government organisations.
Philosophy. Its statement seeking political reform of maternity services is
outlined in the National Maternity Action Plan which was prepared by The
Maternity Coalition in 2002. Membership. As an umbrella organization it has
institutional members from all States and Territories as well as individuals.
Journal. It publishes a quarterly journal entitled "Birth Matters".
