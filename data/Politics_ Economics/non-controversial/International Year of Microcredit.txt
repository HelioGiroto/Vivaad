International Year of Microcredit International Year of Microcredit is a
special event of the United Nations which took place in the year 2005. The
event highlighted microfinance as an instrument for socioeconomic development.
The year was launched on November 18, 2004. Goals. The United Nations Economic
and Social Council proclaimed the year 2005 as the International Year of
Microcredit to call for building inclusive financial sectors and strengthening
the powerful, but often untapped, entrepreneurial spirit existing in
communities around the world.
