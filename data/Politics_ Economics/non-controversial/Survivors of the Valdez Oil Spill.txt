Survivors of the Valdez Oil Spill The group Survivors of the Valdez Oil Spill
claims to represent the 40,000 or so victims of the 1989 Exxon Valdez oil
spill. The organization tracks all information related to the spill and its
aftermath. The organization's website has links to articles, court decisions
and actions related to this incident, as well as articles and reports on the
psychological, economic and environmental impacts of this accident. There is
information for journalists and updates on the ongoing litigation. The site was
created by Steven Goldstein and Jo Miller for the tenth anniversary of the
spill and is no longer being actively updated.
