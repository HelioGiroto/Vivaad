National Association of Old IRA The National Association of Old IRA was a
commemorative organisation made up of members of the Old IRA as opposed to the
then current IRA, which was as now a proscribed organisation. They marched in
1939 in a commemoration of the War of Independence. Aims. As with other
veterans organisations, they looked for the welfare of members, organising
pensions, etc., and commemorating their fallen and battles and events of
significance to membership.
